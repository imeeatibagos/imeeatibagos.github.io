Project Overview

David's Salon faced significant challenges with their outdated booking system, leading to customer inconvenience, overcrowding, and scheduling conflicts. This project addresses these issues by implementing a modern, user-friendly web application that streamlines the entire appointment booking process.

The solution provides customers with the ability to schedule appointments at their preferred times, select specific services and stylists, and receive confirmation notifications. This organized approach eliminates wait times, prevents overbooking, and significantly improves the overall customer experience.
